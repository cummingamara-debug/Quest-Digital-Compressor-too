Image Compression Tips

  • For web use, aim for 60-80% compression for a good balance of quality and size
  • WebP format typically offers better compression than JPG or PNG
  • Larger images can tolerate more compression without visible quality loss
  • Use lower compression (higher quality) for printing purposes
  • Always keep a copy of your original image before compression
